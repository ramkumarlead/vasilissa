Vasilissa Technologies is providing our customers with Mobile development, testing, and maintenance services. We help customers capitalize on the rising mobile market with a comprehensive package with our highly efficient development, testing, and maintenance services.

We design, test and maintain mobile applications for any platform, including iOS, Android, Windows Phone 7 and 8, Symbian and Linux. Upgrades in mobile operating systems require stringent testing in order to conform to more modern device designs. Our testing services include functional testing and cross-platform testing, as well as usability and compatibility testing, localization testing, and field testing. We test our applications across a variety of popular mobile platforms, such as iPhone, Blackberry, Android and Symbian. We study the critical business need of our clients before beginning the initial phases of development. Based on that requirement, we define the systems architecture and integration into existing apps. We ensure that the apps are thoroughly tested before submission to major app stores. We also provide support during the entire product cycle and for regular app maintenance. We constantly enhance our apps with updates and releases to stay up to date.
